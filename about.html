Meet Zak, Alpha, and Gloire

We are three passionate Berea College students — Zak, Alpha, and Gloire — who see the challenges East African students face in pursuing higher education in the U.S. From our own experiences as East Africans, we understand the unique hurdles, and that's why we created Africa Tumaini Academy. Our goal is to provide free resources, mentorship, and guidance on SAT, TOEFL, and Duolingo prep to help African students overcome these barriers and achieve their dreams.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help African students succeed in gaining access to U.S. universities by providing free SAT, TOEFL, and Duolingo prep resources, along with personalized mentorship.

Why We Do This

We believe every African student should have an equal opportunity to pursue education in the U.S. Our goal is to remove the barriers to entry, offering free guidance, materials, and support to help you get there.
