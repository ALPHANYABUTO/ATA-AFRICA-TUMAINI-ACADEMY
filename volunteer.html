Become a Volunteer with Africa Tumaini Academy

We are always looking for passionate and dedicated individuals to help us make a difference in the lives of African students striving to study in the U.S. Whether you're an experienced mentor, content creator, or just someone who wants to contribute, we invite you to join our mission.

As a volunteer, you will play a pivotal role in our programs, helping students navigate the SAT and Duolingo tests, guiding them through the application process, and much more!

Ways You Can Volunteer:

Mentorship

Help guide students through the SAT, TOEFL, or Duolingo test prep and U.S. college applications.

Resource Development

Assist in creating study materials, guides, and practice exams for our students.

Event Organization

Help us organize workshops, webinars, and outreach events for students.

Fundraising

Contribute to raising funds for scholarships and other student needs.

Your involvement will directly impact the educational opportunities available to African students, helping them access U.S. higher education and achieve their dreams!
