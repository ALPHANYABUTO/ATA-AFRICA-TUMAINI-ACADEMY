About Africa Tumaini Academy

Africa Tumaini Academy is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering African students by providing free, high-quality resources and mentorship to help them succeed in gaining access to U.S. universities. Through expert guidance and comprehensive preparation for the SAT and Duolingo English Test, we help students navigate the complex application process with confidence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that African students have the tools, knowledge, and support needed to gain admission to U.S. universities. We offer free SAT and Duolingo prep materials, personalized mentorship, and guidance to help students reach their educational goals.

Why We Do This

We believe that every African student deserves an equal opportunity to pursue higher education abroad. By providing free resources and personalized mentorship, Africa Tumaini Academy removes the financial barriers and logistical challenges that many students face in preparing for U.S. university applications. Our goal is to make U.S. education accessible to all talented African students.
